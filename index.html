Methodology & honesty note. The radar shows illustrative relative positioning under the conditions you select — it is a decision aid, not a head-to-head benchmark run. The fixed figures above are real, from Robbyant's LingBot-Map release (Apr 2026): ~20 FPS streaming, ETH3D F1 of 98.98, and an Oxford Spires absolute trajectory error of 6.42 m (≈2.8× better than the prior best streaming method). The architecture is a Geometric Context Transformer with three attention paths (anchor context, local pose window, trajectory memory). It is weeks old and not yet flight-validated on UAVs — benchmarks are largely ground/handheld — which is exactly why our plan includes running the open-source model over real GPS-denied drone footage as live proof.
